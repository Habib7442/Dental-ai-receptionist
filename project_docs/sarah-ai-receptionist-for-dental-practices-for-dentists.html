Common questions

Questions practice owners ask.

Will Sarah sound robotic to patients?
No. Sarah runs on GPT-4 with voice training tuned for warmth and empathy. Most patients can't tell they're not speaking to a human. You can hear her live by calling our demo line, or test her against any sample script before signing.
Does Sarah integrate with Open Dental, Dentrix, or Eaglesoft?
All plans sync to Google Calendar out of the box. PMS integrations (Open Dental, Dentrix, Eaglesoft) are available on Growth and Multi-location plans, typically rolled out within 1–2 weeks of onboarding.
Is Sarah HIPAA compliant?
Yes. We sign a BAA on request, encrypt all patient data in transit and at rest, and offer US-based data residency on Multi-location plans. Happy to walk your IT or compliance lead through our security stack on a 15-min call.
What if Sarah can't answer something?
She gracefully escalates: takes a detailed message and routes it to your front desk, or transfers live to your on-call line during emergencies. Your patients will never feel stranded.
How long does setup take?
Two business days from contract signed. We handle the call-forwarding setup, calendar sync, voice tuning to your practice tone, and FAQ training. You don't need to lift a finger beyond a 30-min kickoff call.
Will it replace my front desk?
No — and that's the point. Your front desk team is your most valuable patient-facing resource. Sarah handles overflow, after-hours, and routine bookings so your team can focus on patients in the chair, payments, and care coordination.
